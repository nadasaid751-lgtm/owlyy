About the Project

What is SMBOT?

SMBOT is a smart, AI-powered study companion built to transform the way students engage with their learning materials. Instead of spending hours re-reading dense textbooks or lecture notes, you simply upload any PDF and SMBOT instantly gets to work — analyzing the content, understanding the context, and making it interactive for you.

The platform generates clean, intelligent summaries that capture the most important ideas in your document, so you can quickly grasp the big picture without getting lost in details. When you want to go deeper, the interactive Q&A chat lets you have a genuine conversation with your material — ask anything, at any depth level, and get clear, focused answers grounded in your own content.

To make sure you've truly absorbed what you've studied, SMBOT includes an adaptive quiz system that generates multiple-choice questions tailored to your document. The quiz adapts to your performance — if you score well, the difficulty increases; if you struggle, it eases back — ensuring you're always challenged at the right level. Everything you need to study efficiently is in one seamless, beautifully designed experience.
